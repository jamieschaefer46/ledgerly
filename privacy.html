Ledgerly

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Ledgerly stores account, business, transaction, budget, and report data so small business owners can maintain accounting records.

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, business name, password hash, imported bank statement data, account allocations, budgets, and app activity needed to run Ledgerly.

How We Use Information

We use your information to provide the app, secure your account, save your accounting workspace, support subscriptions, improve reliability, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Control

You can export your data or delete your account from Settings. Deletion removes your user, business, transactions, accounts, budgets, reports, and sessions from the active database.

Security

Ledgerly uses password hashing and session cookies. Production deployments must use HTTPS, managed backups, restricted server access, and regular security review.

Important Note

This starter policy must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public launch, especially for POPIA compliance in South Africa.
